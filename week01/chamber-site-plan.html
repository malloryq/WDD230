Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The Cedar Rapids Chamber of Commerce

Selected because it represents the locality and emphasizes the chamber's role in promoting business interests and the area.

Site Purpose

The Cedar Rapids Chamber of Commerce serves as a platform to promote local businesses, attract tourists, and advocate for the community.

1. To promote local businesses and economic growth.

2. To attract tourists and visitors to the area.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What is the history of Cedar Rapids? What are some current events?
